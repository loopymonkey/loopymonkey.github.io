## CREATIVE BRIEF

The first season of Landman I was in charge of the design production for the entire marketing campaign after the hero artwork was finished.  Orchestrated vendor relationships and cross-functional collaboration with global teams to ensure seamless delivery of brand-consistent creative assets across all distribution channels and mediums.

- Key Art
- Promo GFX
- Out of Home print and motion billboards across multiple cities
- Social
- Events
- Digital marketing
