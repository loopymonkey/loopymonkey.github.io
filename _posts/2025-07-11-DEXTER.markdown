## CREATIVE BRIEF

As Design Production Lead for the Dexter: Resurrection, I directed internal design and motion teams alongside external vendor partners to execute the global campaign's visual strategy. Our portfolio encompassed high-profile activations such as Times Square takeovers and branded Amazon shipping boxes.

- Key Art
- Promo GFX
- Out of Home print and motion billboards across multiple cities
- Ancillary Events
- Event signage
- Digital marketing
- Amazon promotional box art
